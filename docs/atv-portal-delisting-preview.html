Model: delisting is a reversible take-down of a published venue — new delisted status + delisted_at/by/reason, a delist change-request type (admin-approved take-down), and self-serve re-list (immediate restore, admin notified). Delisted venues 404 like any unpublished venue and keep their slug. Distinct from draft delete (which is a true, draft-only removal).
