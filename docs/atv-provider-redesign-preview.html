About Park Hyatt Dubai

Set along the tranquil waters of Dubai Creek, Park Hyatt Dubai is a Moorish-inspired retreat of low-rise, whitewashed architecture, palm gardens and waterfront terraces. Its spaces range from an intimate private dining room to a pillar-less ballroom and a signature garden lawn — a favourite for weddings, gala dinners and corporate celebrations that want a resort feel minutes from the city.
