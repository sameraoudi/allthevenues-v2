## 1. What This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy applies to personal information collected through: venue enquiry forms; multi-venue shortlist enquiries; general contact forms; provider or partner enquiry forms; venue listing update requests; website analytics and cookies; email communications with us; and any other form or feature on allthevenues.com that collects information.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party websites, venue provider websites, payment platforms, social media platforms, or other external services linked from our website.

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information.

### 2.1 Information You Provide

When you submit an enquiry or form, we may collect: name; email address; phone number; company name (where applicable); event type; event date or approximate date; guest count; budget range; preferred location; venue preferences; food and beverage requirements; audio-visual or technical requirements; selected or shortlisted venues; message or notes; provider or organisation name (where applicable); website, provider type, and number of venues managed (where applicable); and any other information you choose to provide.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the website, we may collect limited technical information such as: IP address; browser type; device type; pages visited; referring page or source; time and date of visit; interaction with website features; and cookie or analytics identifiers.

This information helps us improve website performance, understand usage, protect the website, and measure enquiry activity.

### 2.3 Information from Venue Providers

If you are a venue provider, we may collect information related to: your organisation; your role or contact details; venues you manage; venue descriptions, images, facilities, and capacity information; listing update requests; provider account activity; enquiries routed to you; and communications with All The Venues.

## 3. How We Use Personal Information

We may use personal information to: respond to enquiries; route event enquiries to relevant venue providers; help users find suitable venues; manage venue shortlists; contact users about their submitted enquiries; contact venue providers about listing requests or provider applications; review, approve, or manage venue listings; maintain and improve website functionality; prevent spam, fraud, misuse, or unauthorised access; send administrative messages; analyse website performance and enquiry trends; maintain business records; and comply with legal or regulatory obligations.

We do not sell personal information to advertisers.

## 4. Sharing Personal Information

We may share personal information in the following situations.

### 4.1 With Venue Providers

If you submit a venue enquiry, we may share relevant enquiry details with selected or suitable venue providers so they can respond to your event request. This may include your name, contact details, event requirements, selected venues, guest count, budget, date, and notes.

### 4.2 With Service Providers

We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate the website, such as: website hosting providers; email delivery services; analytics providers; spam protection providers; security tools; technical support providers; and CRM or lead management tools, if used. These providers may only use the information as needed to provide services to us.

### 4.3 For Legal or Safety Reasons

We may disclose information if required to: comply with applicable laws or legal processes; protect our rights, users, venue providers, or the public; investigate fraud, abuse, security incidents, or misuse; or enforce our Terms of Use.

### 4.4 Business Transfers

If Bianca Event Styling, All The Venues, or relevant business assets are sold, transferred, restructured, or merged, personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to appropriate protections.

## 5. Legal Basis and Consent

We collect and use personal information when there is a valid reason to do so, such as: to respond to your enquiry; to provide the website and related services; to route your enquiry to relevant venue providers, where you have agreed to this; to communicate with you; to protect the website from misuse; to comply with legal obligations; and to pursue legitimate business interests in operating and improving the website.

Where consent is required, we will ask for it through the relevant form or notice. You may withdraw consent where applicable by contacting us.

The website may use cookies and similar technologies to: keep the website working properly; improve user experience; understand website traffic and usage; measure performance of pages and enquiry flows; protect forms from spam or misuse; and remember certain preferences.

We may use analytics tools such as Google Analytics or similar services. These tools may collect information about how users interact with the website.

You can usually control cookies through your browser settings. Some features may not work properly if cookies are disabled. If required, the website should display a cookie notice or preference tool.

## 7. Spam Protection and Security Tools

We may use security and anti-spam tools, such as Cloudflare Turnstile or similar services, to protect forms and prevent automated misuse.

These tools may process technical information such as IP address, browser/device signals, and interaction data to verify that form submissions are legitimate.

We keep personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information.

Examples: enquiry records may be kept for business, reporting, follow-up, and historical tracking purposes; provider application records may be kept to manage provider relationships; technical logs may be kept for security and troubleshooting; and legal or accounting records may be kept where required by law.

When information is no longer needed, we will delete, anonymise, or securely archive it where appropriate.

## 9. Security
