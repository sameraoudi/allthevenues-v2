This Cookie Notice explains how All The Venues (operated by Bianca Event Styling) uses cookies and similar technologies on allthevenues.com. It should be read alongside our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy).

## What are cookies and similar technologies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. "Similar technologies" include things like browser **local storage**, which a website can use to remember small pieces of information on your device. We use only what we need to run the website and understand how it's used.

## What we use

**Essential (required for the site to work).**

- A **session cookie** is used for signed-in administrators and to protect forms against cross-site request forgery (CSRF). This is not used for public browsing.
- **Local storage** is used to remember your **venue shortlist** on your device, so your saved venues persist between visits. This stays on your device and is not an advertising cookie.

**Security / anti-spam.**

- We use **Cloudflare Turnstile** to protect our forms from automated abuse. It may process technical signals (such as IP address and browser/device information) to confirm a submission is legitimate.

**Analytics.**

- We use privacy-friendly analytics to understand how the website is used and to measure enquiry activity, in aggregate. Where analytics tools set cookies or identifiers, they are described in our Privacy Policy.

We do **not** use cookies to sell your data or to serve third-party advertising.

## Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings, and you can clear your shortlist at any time from the shortlist page. Please note that some features — such as remembering your shortlist or signing in to the admin area — may not work properly if you block essential cookies or local storage.

## Third-party services

Some third-party services linked from our website (such as venue provider websites, map services, or social media) may set their own cookies. We are not responsible for those cookies. Please review the relevant third party's cookie or privacy notice.
