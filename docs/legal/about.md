# About All The Venues

All The Venues is a UAE venue discovery and enquiry platform. We help event planners find the right venue for weddings, corporate events, conferences, celebrations, and more — and connect them to the right venue providers through one simple, managed enquiry.

## What we do

We bring the UAE's event venues together in one curated, easy-to-browse place. You can explore venues by event type, location, guest count, and budget, compare the details that matter, shortlist your favourites, and send a single enquiry — rather than chasing venues one by one.

## Curated, not a directory dump

We focus on presenting venues clearly and credibly. Listings are organised, searchable, and kept meaningful, with a small number of trust signals — such as **Verified** and **Featured** — rather than a wall of badges. Our goal is a premium, calm experience that helps you find the right venue faster and trust the result.

## How our enquiry model works

When you enquire, your details go to All The Venues, and we route your request to the right venue providers on your behalf. You share your details once; we help you reach the venues that fit. Public venue pages never expose a provider's direct contact details — the enquiry is always managed through ATV.

## Value for event planners

- Browse and compare UAE venues in one place.
- Shortlist multiple venues and enquire about them together.
- Share your details once and let us help you reach suitable providers.
- No account required to browse, shortlist, or enquire.

## Value for venue providers

- Qualified, structured enquiries with the details that matter — event type, date, guest count, and budget.
- Your direct contact details stay private; enquiries are routed through ATV.
- A curated platform to showcase your venues, with Verified and Featured options.
